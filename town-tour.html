To the Beach!

Folkestone beaches are predominately pebbled. As the tide goes out, they expose a lot of rock pools, making it a fun day out for families to explore the outdoors. A popular location is The Warren where it allows dogs on the beach all year around. Click on the pictures to find the location.

To the High Street!

A.K.A. The Creative Quarter, is a vibrant part of town filled with colours and a concentration of independent shops, cafes and restaurants. It's the perfect spot for gift shopping or to take a piece of Folkestone with you.

The Old High Street The Old High Street

Our town is proud to support local businesses. You will notice no multinational chains on The Old High Street, only family run businesses. Support our town, shop Local.
