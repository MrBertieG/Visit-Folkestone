To the Beach!

Folkestone beaches are predominately pebbled. As the tide goes out, they expose a lot of rock pools, making it a fun day out for families which enjoy outdoor exploring. A popular location is The Warren where it allows dogs on the beach all year around. Click on the pictures to find the location.
